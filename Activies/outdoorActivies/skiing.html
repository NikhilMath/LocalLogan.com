Skiing/Snowboarding

Utah is known to have the greatest snow on Earth, so you get your moneys worth out of the city and enjoy the snow!

Nordic United this is FREE. Nordic united grooms the snow for skiers in the winter at green canyon. . While it is not as nice as an actual ski resort, and only gets groomed a couple times a week, it is free to try out. You can snoe shoe to the top and ski down and it's a nice little trail. Especially if you are new or have never done this before, this is a EXCELLENT beginners course. Personally, I have only ever done this twice and I rented my equipment at Al’s Sporting goods store.
